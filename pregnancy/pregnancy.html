Pregnancy

After fertilization of an egg cell by a sperm cell, a woman will become pregnant. During this 9 month journey, many changes will happen.

First Trimester (Months 1 - 3)

Month 1

  • The placenta forms. The placenta is an organ that helps transfer food in to the fetus and waste out.
  • A face somewhat forms. There are circles for where the eyes will be, and a mouth, jaw, and throat are forming.
  • The baby is the size of a grain of rice.

Month 2

  • Ears, arms, legs, fingers, toes and eyes all are forming.
  • The brain and other parts of nervous system are developed. The digestive tract and sensory organs start to form.
  • The embryo is able to move.
  • The baby is about an inch long.

Month 3

  • The baby is fully formed. Nails are forming, as well as the external ears.
  • The reproductive organs have developed.
  • The circulatory and urinary systems (breathing and peeing) are working.
  • The baby is 3 - 4 inches long.

Second Trimester (Months 4 - 6)

Month 4

  • The baby will move around a lot.
  • The brain structures develop more prominently. The baby also becomes more sensitve to touch!
  • The baby is about 8 inches long.

Month 5

  • The baby's heart is beating very strongly and can be heard with a stethoscope.
  • THe baby will be covered in a thin layer of hair, as well as hair on it's head and eyebrows.
  • You can tell the gender of the baby.
  • THe baby is about a foot long.

Month 6

  • The baby is becoming fatter, especially in the butt and shoulders.
  • The lungs are still not stong enough for the outside world.
  • The baby is about 12.5 inches long.

Third Trimester (Months 7 - 9)

Month 7

  • The baby is able to use all it's senses. It's eyes are open, and is able to swallow.
  • The baby now has a layer of fat.
  • The stomach and intestines are functional.
  • THe baby is much more energetic than before.
  • The baby is about 15 inches long.

Month 8

  • The baby now looks like a chubby newborn.
  • The baby is sensitive to pain and has a sense of taste.
  • The baby is about 1.5 feet long.

Month 9

  • The baby is ready to be born. It is slowly dropping down into the mother's pelvis.
  • The baby is still gaining weight and all of it's organs are mature, including it's lungs.
  • The baby is about 19.5 inches long.

Birth

After 9 months, the baby is born!
