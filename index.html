About Us

The Birds and the Bees is a website resource developed to help teach young adults comprehensive sexual education. It is designed by a group of young adults for young adults to help reach out to the up and coming generations in a more relatable manner. We are a team of college students working on this project for our INFO 200 course. Our goal is to spread accurate, uniform and comprehensive sexual education throughout the nation.

Have any Questions?

Don't sweat it. We're here to offer a comprehensive sexual education course that will answer most common questions about anything sex-related. If you still have unanswered questions, head over to the question board to anonymously post a question. We'll get back to it ASAP!

Great for teachers!

This resource provides a simple and elegant solution to an awkward topic. We know that many teachers loathe the day they'll have to find a way to reach a younger generation with imperative information. But now you don't have to worry about it! Our website will do all of the heavy lifting for you, and without any awkwardness!
