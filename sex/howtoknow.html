Are you ready for sex?

Whether or not you're ready to have sex can depend on a number of factors. When deciding whether to have sex, you should consider what your values are and what is important to you.

If you think you're ready for sex, don't forget that sex is a two-way road. You have to make sure that your partner is ready to take this step too. Keep in mind that this is important to them as well—don't take their values for granted. Read ahead for more information on how to have a safe and healthy sex life.

If you don't think you're ready yet, that's completely fine! Sex is a big step and shouldn't be taken lightly. It's perfectly normal to have your own reasons for not being sexually active. You don't need to explain yourself to others. If you want to wait before having sex, click the button below to read more about abstinence.
