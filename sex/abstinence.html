Abstinence

Sex sounds great, but don't forget that the best way to stay sexually healthy is to remain abstinent.

What is abstinence?
Abstinence is not having sex.

Why should I be abstinent?
If two people don't have sex, then there is no chance of someone getting pregnant or getting an STI (Sexually Transmitted Infection), including HIV. Anyone can practive abstinence, you, your friends, even your parents!

Does it work?
Abstinence works 100% of the time in preventing pregnancy and STI's, including HIV. Although other forms of birth control work very well, none of them work as well as abstinence does!
