Consider your values.

Sex is a beautiful thing. Don't forget to think about all the different values that play a part in it too!

What values are we talking about?
Religious, family, and individual values play a big part in your sexual decisions.

Family & Religion
Some religions urge that you stay abstinent until marriage. Your family may follow the same values as a certain religion. If you choose to follow their advice, that is great! However, don't feel down if you decide not too. Everyone is free to make their own choices. The best thing you can do is be sure that your's is informed and thought-out.

Individual
Ultimately it comes down to what you believe is the right thing to do. What are your values?
