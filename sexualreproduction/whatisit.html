Sexual Reproduction

What is it?

Sexual reproduction is a necessary cause in continuing the human existence. As many people say, it occurs when two people love each other very much. Sometimes it even happens unexepectedly. In either case, it is when a woman and man partake in sexual intercourse (have sex, do it, make love) and have a child that is a unique combination of the parent's genes.

The Process

During sexual reproduction, the man release sperm while inside the woman. Many sperm go through a long process, searching for an egg to fertilize. In many cases, the egg will not be fertilized. But in the case when an egg lets the sperm in, a single-celled zygote is formed containing DNA from the male's sperm and the female's ovum (or egg).

A little confused?

Don't worry, you're not the only one. Sex is a complicated process. But it can also be very exciting and even fun! The truth is, most of what you need to know to have a safe and healthy sex life is much simpler.
